# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in **Flare** and its community a harassment-free experience for everyone.  
We expect all contributors, maintainers, and users to respect this pledge.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, abuse, or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Publishing others’ private information without consent
- Any other behavior that could harm the community or project

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting **[xafans@outlook.com]**.  
All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and will result in an appropriate response.

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct in all spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community forums.

## Reporting Guidelines
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
1. Contact the project maintainers via the email above.
2. Include relevant details such as links, screenshots, or descriptions.
3. Maintain confidentiality as appropriate.
